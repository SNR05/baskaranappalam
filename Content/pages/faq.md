## How to fry Appalam?
Heat oil to ~180°C. Fry each appalam 2–3 seconds per side until it puffs and turns golden. Drain on tissue.

## Can I roast without oil?
Yes. Roast over low flame with tongs until it puffs. Keep moving to avoid burning.

## Shelf life & storage
Unfried appalams last 6–9 months in a cool, dry place. Keep airtight to prevent moisture.

## Ingredients & allergens
Urad flour, rice flour, salt, edible oil, baking soda, asafoetida (hing). Contains asafoetida.
